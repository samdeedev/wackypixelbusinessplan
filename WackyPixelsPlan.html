2. Community & Events Strategy

Using events not just for ticket sales, but as Lead Magnets and Portfolio Builders.

The Silent Cinema Party

(Formerly Listening Party)

Concept: A Silent Disco with a visual twist.

Strategy: Held at a partner venue (Warehouse/Outdoor). We use our high-end projection and cinema cameras to project live visuals. We film it like a music video.

Monetization: Tickets + Sponsorships + The "After-Movie" Portfolio piece.

The Gear & Grade Sessions

(Creatives Hangout)

Concept: A "Touch & Try" technical masterclass at our office.

Strategy: Invite freelancers to test our industry-standard gear. We position Wacky Pixels as the "Authority" with the best tools.

Goal: Build a freelance network that hires US when they need high-end gear for their projects.

Pop-Up Runway Productions

(Fashion Show)

Concept: High-end fashion showcase.

Strategy: We partner with a luxury hotel for the space. We provide the Production (Lights, Cameras, Livestream). We trade our "Tech" for their "Venue."

Goal: Client acquisition for Fashion Commercials.
